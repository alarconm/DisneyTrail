## PRIORITY 1: KARAOKE MINI-GAME (NEW!)
*Kristin loves karaoke - this is a must-have!*

### Concept: "Disney Karaoke Challenge"
A rhythm-based singing game where lyrics scroll and you time button presses to the beat.

### Features:
- **5-8 Disney Songs** with scrolling lyrics
  - "Let It Go" (Frozen)
  - "A Whole New World" (Aladdin)
  - "Under the Sea" (Little Mermaid)
  - "Hakuna Matata" (Lion King)
  - "How Far I'll Go" (Moana)
  - "Part of Your World" (Little Mermaid)
  - "Be Our Guest" (Beauty and the Beast)
  - "Colors of the Wind" (Pocahontas)

### Mechanics:
- Lyrics scroll across screen karaoke-style
- Hit zones appear on key syllables
- Press SPACE or tap to "sing" in time
- Perfect/Good/Miss timing feedback
- Crowd reaction animations (cats watching!)
- Score based on accuracy
- Rewards: Morale boost + special "Karaoke Star" achievement

### Visual Design:
- Stage with spotlight
- Microphone emoji animation
- The three cats as audience (different reactions)
- Disney-style sparkles on perfect hits
- Song title card with character silhouette
